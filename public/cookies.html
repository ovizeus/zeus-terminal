Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025 — Private Beta

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, from keeping you logged in to tracking browsing behavior.

2. Our Cookie Usage

Zeus Terminal uses exactly one cookie.

It is strictly necessary for authentication. We do not use any analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party cookies.

3. Cookie Details

Name Type Purpose Attributes
zeus_token Strictly necessary Stores your encrypted session token to keep you authenticated after login. Without this cookie, you cannot access the platform. httpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax

4. What We Do NOT Use

  • No analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • No tracking or profiling cookies
  • No advertising or remarketing cookies
  • No third-party cookies of any kind
  • No social media pixels or widgets
  • No fingerprinting or similar tracking technologies

5. Why No Consent Banner

Under GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive, strictly necessary cookies — those essential for a service explicitly requested by the user (such as authentication) — are exempt from consent requirements. Since our only cookie falls into this category, no cookie consent banner is required or displayed.

6. Managing Cookies

You can delete or block cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that blocking the zeus_token cookie will prevent you from logging into Zeus Terminal.

7. Changes

If we ever introduce additional cookies (for example, for analytics or preferences), this policy will be updated accordingly and consent will be obtained where required by law.

8. Contact

Questions about our cookie usage: support@zeus-terminal.com
