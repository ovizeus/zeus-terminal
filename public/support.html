Before Contacting Support

  • If you just registered, your account requires manual administrator approval. This is not automatic — please allow time for review.
  • For 2FA issues, check your spam/junk folder for the verification email.
  • For exchange API issues, verify your API key permissions and IP whitelist settings on your exchange.

Important Notes

  • Zeus Terminal is in private beta. Features and availability may change without notice.
  • Support is provided on a reasonable-effort basis. There are no guaranteed response times or SLAs during the beta period.
  • We will never ask for your password, 2FA codes, or exchange API secrets via email.
